Complex Manager's Report

July is pretty slow here at the club! Don't forget to stop in for a sandwich or fish (on Fridays) or a salad during these hot summer months. Enjoy the patio!

We have started the "Queen of Hearts" at the club. The first drawing was Tuesday, April 24th. We will have the drawing every Tuesday at 7:30 p.m. Tickets are $1.00 each. You must be a member to purchase a ticket. Hopefully this builds up our Tuesday nights and our memberships.

Saturday, October 20th - Nite at the Races - WestStark as our Co-sponsor. Tickets will be available in September.

Bloodmobile will be here Wednesday, July 18th.

Bingo continues every Monday, with doors opening at 4:30 p.m. Our crowds are maintaining their numbers. C'mon out and play bingo to support us. Thanks to all the new volunteers for Bingo who have given up a Monday or two to help us out. We appreciate all of the new and of course all of you who give up every Monday out of your busy schedules. We couldn't keep bingo going without all of you, so thank you!

Texas Hold'Em every Thursday, at 7:00 p.m. We sure can use some more players, only averaging around 20-24 people. Buy in is $20.00 and a $5.00 optional high hand. 100% PAYOUTS. Try It, come out and play some time, would love to see some new faces.
