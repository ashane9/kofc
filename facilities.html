The K-Club

Newly renovated, the clubroom provides the perfect atmosphere for Knights and Social members to mingle. Centralized around the bar, the room offers plenty of seating within view of a hd television.

Club Hours:
Mon, Tues, Wed: 4-11 PM
Thurs, Fri, Sat: 4-12 AM

Dining Room Hours:
Wed, Thurs, Sat: 5-8 PM
Fri: 4-8 PM

Photos


The Nina Room

The smallest of the rooms with a capacity of 80, the Nina is perfect for family dinners and small events. The Nina is also used for council meetings.

Photos


The Pinta Room

The Pinta room was recently renovated to bring a more modern environment for any event. The room has a capacity of 250 but boasts the feeling of a much larger space. And with its own bar and proximity to the other rooms, the Pinta can fit the needs of almost any event.

Photos


The Santa Maria Room

The largest of the rooms, the Santa Maria can accommodate up to 600 guests. The spacious room is perfect for large receptions or conferences and includes a large bar area and quick access to the other rooms in the building.

Photos


Pavilion

Newly renovated to be fully enclosed, the pavilion is perfect for any outdoor event. The pavilion offers plenty of space, a full-sized kitchen and access to a large field with the option to open or leave the garage doors closed.
