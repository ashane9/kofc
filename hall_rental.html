Hall Rental Information

The Knights of Columbus Hall is the perfect location for celebrating special occasions. Conveniently located just West of downtown Massillon, offers spacious, clean and ideal accommodations for your event, whether it's for 50 or 500 guests.



  • Wedding Receptions - 150 to 500 capacity
  • Meeting/Conference Facility
  • Health Fairs
  • Tradeshows
  • Class Reunions
  • Club Banquets

    • Catering

    The Knights of Columbus Hall provide catering, tables, chairs, linens, adequate dance floor/band area, parking for 330 vehicles, full bar service, coat rooms available, beautiful wooded grove for photos.



    Prices - Available upon request by calling, 330-837-9101 and ask for the complex manager who will work with you in help coordinating your special event.

    The Knights of Columbus Hall has our own caterer who will work with you or your event planner to choose the perfect menu for your event. Whether you are planning a sit down dinner or buffet style meal, K of C has exactly what you're looking for.

    Sit Down Dinners
    Include (but are not limited to): File Mignon, Cornish Hen, Lobster, Chicken Cordon Bleu, Ham, Stuffed Pork Chops and Broiled Scrod.

    Buffet Meal:
    Your choice of 3 meats, from - Baked Chicken, Italian Sausage w/peppers & onions, Roast Beef, Baked Ham, or Turkey Roll-ups
    Choice of Potato - Mashed with beef/chicken gravy, Augratin, Scalloped, Parsley Buttered Red Skin Potatoes
    Choice of Vegetable
    Hot Pasta
    Rolls/Butter
    Coffee, Tea, Ice Tea, Milk

    Dessert
    Only included with sit down dinners.

    Sales Tax and 15% Gratuity will be added to all dinner & buffet prices.

    Linens (choice of colors) are included in the price for both sit down dinners and buffet meals.
